What is Personal Accident Insurance?

Personal Accident Insurance provides financial protection against accidental injuries, disabilities, and death. It covers medical expenses, hospitalization, and offers compensation for loss of income due to accidents, ensuring your family's financial stability during tough times.

Types of Personal Accident Insurance

Individual Accident Policy

Coverage for an individual against accidental injuries and death.

  • Personal coverage
  • Disability benefits
  • Medical expenses
  • Death benefit

Family Accident Policy

Comprehensive protection for the entire family under a single policy.

  • Family coverage
  • Spouse and children
  • Group benefits
  • Flexible sum insured

Group Accident Policy

Coverage for employees or members of an organization.

  • Corporate coverage
  • Bulk discounts
  • Employee benefits
  • Easy enrollment

Student Accident Policy

Specialized coverage for students against accidental injuries at school or college.

  • Student coverage
  • Educational institutions
  • Accidental death
  • Disability benefits

Travel Accident Policy

Protection against accidents during travel, both domestic and international.

  • Travel coverage
  • Medical evacuation
  • Emergency assistance
  • Worldwide protection

Permanent Disability Policy

Coverage for permanent disabilities resulting from accidents.

  • Permanent disability
  • Lump sum benefit
  • Rehabilitation expenses
  • Income replacement
