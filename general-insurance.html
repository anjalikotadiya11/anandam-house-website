General Insurance

Comprehensive Protection for Your Assets & Well-being

What is General Insurance?

General Insurance provides protection against financial losses arising from damage to property, vehicles, health, and other assets. Unlike life insurance, it covers non-life risks and offers compensation for losses due to accidents, natural disasters, theft, and other unforeseen events.

Types of General Insurance

Health Insurance

Comprehensive medical coverage for individuals and families, including hospitalization, surgeries, and preventive care.

  • Hospitalization expenses
  • Pre and post-hospitalization
  • Day care procedures
  • Critical illness coverage

Motor Insurance

Protection for your vehicles against accidents, theft, and third-party liabilities as per legal requirements.

  • Comprehensive coverage
  • Third-party liability
  • Own damage protection
  • Personal accident cover

Property Insurance

Covers damage to your home, office, or commercial property due to fire, theft, and natural disasters.

  • Building structure
  • Contents coverage
  • Natural calamities
  • Burglary protection

Travel Insurance

Protection during domestic and international travel, covering medical emergencies and trip-related issues.

  • Medical emergencies
  • Trip cancellation
  • Baggage loss
  • Flight delays

Personal Accident

Financial protection against accidental injuries, disabilities, and death with comprehensive coverage.

  • Accidental death
  • Permanent disability
  • Medical expenses
  • Weekly compensation

Liability Insurance

Protects against legal liabilities arising from third-party claims for damages or injuries.

  • Public liability
  • Professional indemnity
  • Product liability
  • Employer's liability
