Term Insurance

Affordable Pure Life Protection for Your Family

What is Term Insurance?

Term Insurance is a pure protection life insurance policy that provides high coverage at affordable premiums for a specific period. It offers financial security to your family in case of your untimely demise during the policy term, with no maturity benefits if you survive the term.

Types of Term Insurance

Level Term Insurance

Fixed sum assured throughout the policy term with consistent premium payments.

  • Fixed coverage amount
  • Level premiums
  • Simple structure
  • Easy to understand

Increasing Term Insurance

Sum assured increases over time to keep pace with inflation and growing needs.

  • Increasing coverage
  • Inflation protection
  • Growing family needs
  • Higher premiums

Decreasing Term Insurance

Sum assured decreases over time, typically used to cover reducing liabilities.

  • Decreasing coverage
  • Lower premiums
  • Liability coverage
  • Mortgage protection

Return of Premium Term

Returns all premiums paid if you survive the policy term.

  • Premium return
  • Higher premiums
  • Survival benefit
  • Forced savings

Convertible Term Insurance

Option to convert to permanent life insurance without medical examination.

  • Conversion option
  • No medical tests
  • Future flexibility
  • Higher initial cost

Rider Enhanced Term

Basic term insurance with additional riders for comprehensive protection.

  • Critical illness rider
  • Accidental death rider
  • Disability rider
  • Enhanced protection
