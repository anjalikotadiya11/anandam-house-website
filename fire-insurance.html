Protection Against Fire Damage to Your Property

What is Fire Insurance?

Fire Insurance is a specialized insurance policy that provides financial protection against losses caused by fire and related perils. It covers damage to buildings, contents, machinery, and other property due to fire, lightning, explosion, and other fire-related incidents.

Types of Fire Insurance

Standard Fire Policy

Basic fire insurance covering damage due to fire, lightning, and explosion.

  • Fire damage
  • Lightning strikes
  • Explosion coverage
  • Basic perils

Comprehensive Fire Policy

Extended coverage including additional perils like natural calamities and riots.

  • Natural calamities
  • Riot and strike
  • Malicious damage
  • Extended perils

Industrial Fire Policy

Specialized coverage for industrial units, factories, and manufacturing facilities.

  • Factory buildings
  • Machinery coverage
  • Raw materials
  • Finished goods

Home Fire Policy

Comprehensive protection for residential properties and household contents.

  • Building structure
  • Household contents
  • Personal belongings
  • Additional living expenses

Commercial Fire Policy

Protection for commercial establishments, offices, and retail spaces.

  • Office buildings
  • Commercial equipment
  • Inventory coverage
  • Business interruption

Consequential Loss Policy

Covers loss of profits and additional expenses due to fire-related business interruption.

  • Loss of profits
  • Fixed expenses
  • Additional expenses
  • Business continuity
