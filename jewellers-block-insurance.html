Jewellers Block Insurance

Specialized Protection for Jewelry Businesses & Valuables

What is Jewellers Block Insurance?

Jewellers Block Insurance is a specialized insurance policy designed specifically for jewelry businesses, goldsmiths, and individuals dealing with precious metals and gemstones. It provides comprehensive coverage against theft, burglary, fire, and other risks associated with handling valuable jewelry and precious items.

Types of Coverage

Stock-in-Trade Coverage

Protects jewelry inventory, raw materials, and finished products in your business premises.

  • Gold and silver jewelry
  • Precious gemstones
  • Raw materials
  • Finished products

Transit Insurance

Covers jewelry while being transported from one location to another, including courier and personal transport.

  • Road transport
  • Air freight
  • Courier services
  • Personal carrying

Showroom Coverage

Protects jewelry displayed in showrooms, exhibitions, and temporary locations.

  • Display items
  • Exhibition coverage
  • Temporary locations
  • Showroom fixtures

Work-in-Progress

Covers jewelry items that are in the process of being manufactured or repaired.

Safe Deposit Coverage
